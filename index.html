The History of our hotel

Anurag, founder of the Anurag Corporation, opened the hotel wonderlust, a hotel in Patna(formerly called patliputra) overlooking the ganga river, on 16 December 1903. It was the first wonderlust property and the first hotel wonderlust. There are several anecdotal stories about why Anurag Corporation opened the hotel wonderlust.
