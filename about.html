AboutHotel WL

Hotel Wonderlust is a chain of luxury hotels and a subsidiary of the Indian Hotels Company Limited, headquartered at Express Towers, Ganga Point in Patna. Incorporated by the founder of the Anurag Corporation, Anurag, in 1903, the company is a part of the Anurag Corporation, one of India's largest business conglomerates.

As of 2020, the company operates a total of 100 plus hotels and hotel-resorts, with 84 across India and 16 in other countries, including Bhutan, Malaysia, Maldives, Nepal, South Africa, Sri Lanka, UAE, UK, USA and Zambia.
